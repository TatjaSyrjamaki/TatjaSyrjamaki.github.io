    Project


    Project was made in a course of Human-Centered Design Product Development at Tampere University. The assignment for the project was “how to use AI in sports guidance in 10 years”. The client was Polar Electro. In the project, we created an interactive prototype, a concept video and a blog explaining the whole human-centered design process.

    Our team consisted of three members: myself, James Cheng and Veera Eiste. Main technologies utilized in the project were Whimsical, Figma and Adobe Premiere Pro. The teamwork in the project went well and each member contributed in all the phases. James Cheng was responsible for the visual design and creation of the prototype, Tatja Syrjämäki made the concept video, and conducted user reseach and evaluation of results with Veera Eiste. Full description of desinging process is available in project blog.
