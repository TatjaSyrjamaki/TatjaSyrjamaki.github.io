    Story behind the game

    "Good games have always a good story". The story of this game starts, when little fish Tim is relaxly swimming on the home coral reefs. Suddenly a hungry shark arrives on the scene and want to eat the small fish. Tim need to to swim away from the home reefs and watch out for other coral reefs creatures. The goal of the game is to swim away sharks and look out obstacles in the water and stay alive as long as possible.
