Adventure game HuiHai


About the game


Game name: HuiHai Number of players: 1 The story of the game Little fish Tim is swimming on the coral reef, but soon a hungry shark arrives on the scene and tries to eat the small fish. Tim need to to swim away and watch out for other coral dwellers. The idea and goals of the game The goal of the game is to swim away sharks and dodge obstacles in the water and stay alive for a long time.
